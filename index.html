📖 Pleas read the rules listed below carefully!! 📖

  • The game has two players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he wants. Each result get added to his Round score.
  • BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his Round score gets lost. After that it's the next player's turn.
  • The player can choose to 'Hold' which means his round score gets added to his Global score. After that it's the next player's turn.
  • The first player to reach the entered points in Global score wins the game.
